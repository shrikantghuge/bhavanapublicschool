Hostel

The child is informally educated by living with others and the very process of living together educates. It enlarges and enlightens experiences, it stimulates and enriches imagination, it creates responsibility: accuracy and vividness of statement and thought. The school hostel symbolizes the magical power inherent in an environment of togetherness, which instills in the child feeling of affection, essence of protection and an opportunity for socialization.

The full fledged hostel with facilities to accommodate five hundred students functions in the school campus. There are separate hostels for boys and girls. The residents are provided to tier cots with blankets and bed sheets. Sanitation is adequate with bores dug in the compound taking care of water equipments. Hot water provided for the residents to take bath in the morning.

Medical Facility

The BAMS Medical College functioning in the school compounds takes care of the medical needs. The doctor, nurse and attendants of the hospital offer their services 24 hours a day. The health of the students is monitored regularly and remedial measures are taken. School can avail the service of an ambulance cum mobile clinic attached to the hospital in case of emergencies.

Refectory

The facility provides a vegetarian diet of high nutritive value. Milk is given early in the mooring and before going to bed. On Sundays and festive occasions, sweets and fruits are served.

Communication Facility

Telephone and internet facilities in the hostel enable parents to communicate with their children. Children can be contacted on phone every Sunday between 10 am and 4 pm. Visiting time: Parents can meet their children on the last Sunday of every month from 9 am to 4 pm.
