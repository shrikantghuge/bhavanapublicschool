Sports

It is always said that  A Sound Mind Dwells in a Sound Body Thus true education aims at developing the all round personality of students & for all round development of personality the curriculum should include sports & games as far a health development is concerned.

Sports calibrate a students mind, body and soul. Sipna has mixture of sports and studies which make Bhavana Public School students different. Students of our school participate in each & every sport activity. Students from our school had always been a part of various sports organized at various level. We have college team for various sports like foot ball, cricket, basket ball, volley ball kabaddi etc.

Yogasana

The ultimate goal of Yoga is liberation though the exact definition of what form this takes depends on the philosophical or theological system with which it is conjugated.To make minds ready for everything, Yoga and exercise is much important. We provide the best platform for all the students which help them to make all-over development.

Accordingly, "Yoga" has five principal meanings:
1. yoga as a disciplined method for attaining a goal.
2. yoga as techniques of controlling the body and the mind.
3.yoga as a name of one of the schools or systems of philosophy (darśana).
4. yoga in connection with other words, such as "hatha-, mantra-, and laya-," referring to traditions specialising in particular techniques of yoga.
5. yoga as the goal of yoga practice.
