About Bhavana Public School

Welcome to Bhavana Public School (CBSE)

Bhavana Public School (BPS) is CBSE School belongs to (Bhavana Group), Bhavana Public School, Degaon, established in the year 2003, led by highly qualified and dedicated teachers from Kerala, enjoys the pride of place in the educational sphere of Vidarbha region. The school recognizes the fact that the great advances in science and technology and the rapid expansion of knowledge, it has become imperative that learning becomes specific and well-defined to enable students to acquire the professional knowledge required to survive in this increasing competitive world. We understand that the most important part of education is to inculcate in students the spirit the spirit of ‘we can do it’. The confidence of the students who have been with us is greater than ever. Our contentment lies in seeing the child step out of the school with feet firmly on the ground, the mind fearless and head held high.

Aims and Objective

The school works on the principle: “Yesterday's child, today's student and tomorrow's citizen”. A responsible citizen is the pillar of the great nation. We promise to transform today's student into a responsible and responsive citizen.

The school provides with all prospects of knowledge for the all round development of the personality of students.

We promise to educate students of the age group 2.5 to 16 years with broad spectrumed, profession-oriented education to achieve the goal of moulding the future citizens of India.

We promise to create responsible future citizens with the highest physical, moral and cultural values.

We promise to prove by act that “discipline in life makes a person perfect and self-sufficient.

Curriculum

    Bhavana Public School is an English Medium School recognised by the Maharashtra School Education Department, Mumbai and affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. The curriculum of the school is designed as per the rules of Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The academic year, which begins in June and culminates with April, consists of 2 Unit Tests, First Semester and Second Semester Examinations. . The academic year is divided into two sessions:
    • First session - June to November
    • Second session - November to April
